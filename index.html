About James

05.13.16

My name is James Steimel and I am software engineer currently residing in south Saint Louis City. I am currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science from the University of Missouri - Saint Louis. I have always been interested in technology, and computers in particular. I have fond memories of my father teaching me as a child how to use DOS on our IBM PCjr. I enjoy learning new computer languages, as well as developing various applications and documents. When I'm not coding, I'm most likely spending time with my loving wife, Amanda, or my supportive family. My hobbies include reading and playing piano.
